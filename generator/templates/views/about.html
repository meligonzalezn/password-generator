About

Make sure your password is strong and secure. A good password also consists of capital letters, special character such as #@&% and numbers. Also, it is important to mention that the longer the password, the safer.

Weak passwords are easily cracked. That is why you should try to create a strong password:

  • Passwords should be long.
  • Passwords should contain at least 12 characters
  • Passwords should contain at least 1 uppercase letter
  • Passwords should contain at least 1 lowercase letter
  • Passwords should contain at least 1 numerical character
  • Symbol characters are encouraged (e.g. @#$%^&!.)

With this application you can generate a safe password of maximum 20 characters with capital letters, special characters and numbers.
